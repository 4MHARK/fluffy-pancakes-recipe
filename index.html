Fluffy Pancakes

A quick recipe for light, Fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Details

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 10 minutes
  • Serving: 4 pancakes
  • Difficulty: 10 easy

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • pinch of salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in another bowl.
  3. Pour wet into dry and stir until just combined (don't overmix).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour 1/4 cup batter; cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm

Tips

Tip: For fluffier pancakes, let the batter rest for 5 minutes. Do not overmix.
